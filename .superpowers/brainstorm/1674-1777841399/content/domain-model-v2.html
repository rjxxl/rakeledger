Note on "other" / collateral

The watch-as-payment situation is modeled as a Marker with the new collateral field — not as a separate account. The player owes cash; the watch is held as security. When they pay cash → marker.repaid_amount goes up, watch returned. If they don't pay → marker.status = written_off, room keeps the watch.
