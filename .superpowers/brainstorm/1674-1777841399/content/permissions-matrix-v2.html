Session-open behavior (updated):
  • All account balances start at $0 when a session opens.
  • Cashier can optionally enter a starting float (e.g., $500 in small bills for change). Recorded as Session.opening_cash + an opening transaction.
  • At close-out, the float is recovered before final reconciliation; Session.closing_cash is recorded.
  • Sessions are now fully self-contained — no cross-session coupling. Tonight's books don't depend on what last night left in the drawer.
